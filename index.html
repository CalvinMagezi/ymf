About

Teamwork makes the dream work

Our ultimate goal is to make use of the 2 square miles acquired by Mr Yesero Mugenyi for the undertaking of various projects, to create a production-satellite town with the capacity for commercial, industrial, nature reserve and other productions, distinct from that of the parent town, - a new Kikwite Town in Hoima District

This will be achieved by our undertaking of various projects, some of which are already in motion, including building; a golf course, hotel and accomodation, hospital and medical facilities, game reserve and animal conservation facilities, shopping centers, a sports arena, schools and universities etc. (Find full project list further below)

Location

Hoima District

The Yesero Mugenyi Foundation was founded by Mr Yesero Mugenyi, and shall be carried on by his descendants. Together we have committed to build an entirely self sufficient Satellite Town upon the 2 square miles of land Mr Yesero Mugenyi labored so hard to aquire for the sake of his children and grandchildren.

The Y.M.F is currently being pushed forward by Mr Yesero Mugenyi’s youngest daughter, Ms Ada Mugenyi. Alongside her siblings, her children, her nieces and nephews: we shall all be working hand in hand to accomplish launching all our projects by 2030.

Hospital Project

Working along with the technology project, we intend to equip ourselves with state of the art medical facilities. With the sorrowful damage the COVD-19 pandemic has caused to the world, Africa has witnessed how hopelessly behind we are in terms of medical services.

We shall partner with top medical companies to provide our Satellite Town with state of the art medical facilities and well trained doctors, aiming to help bridge that gap between the medical facilities in Uganda and those of 1st world countries.

Our hospital shall go hand in hand with our non-profit organization called the “Byron Magezi Sickle Cell Foundation”

Agri Tourism Project

The Y.M.F already owns a dairy farm that has been functional for years. Alongside this, we are starting smaller projects such us; rabbit, pigeon, goat, turkey and chicken farming.

We plan to plant a wide variety of plants that shall act as medicinal, aroma therapeutic, visually pleasing and vital for the future to come.

These projects will be used to educate interested groups, as agriculture was not only with us from the dawn of civilization, but shall be our corner stone for future generations to come.

Eco Tourism Project

We will build a green lodge near the Game Reserve to support Eco Tourism and Bird Watching. Uganda is home to over 1000 bird species.

You can find the latest updated list here: birds of Uganda

We will use the Green Lodge and Eco Tourism to promote recycling, starting with the very construction of the lodge which will be made from materials we have planted ourselves (eg. Bamboo) and will be fully equipped with the latest green technology, allowing visitors to have a unique experience that they will never forget.

We will equip each of our lodges with a bird encyclopaedia and bird watching station, allowing families to view the wide variety of birds and other animals within the area.

Game Reserve / Conservation

The Y.M.F land contains 300 acres of forest area, which will be entirely closed off for a game reserve and animal conservation.

The Uganda Tourism Board shall be approached to work alongside the ZTA (Zimbabwe Tourism Authority) on which species of animals can be relocated to this conservation area.

This will raise tourism and we will use our profits to fund further animal conservations throughout other game safaris.

Schools / Universities

We intend to build high class and affordable educational facilities all the way from primary to university level. In doing so we will be able to educate the locals and allow them to build their own households and smaller communities. We will also open up all education to international students and allow foreign students to experience the future of learning.

We will implement modern day teaching methods and ensure our education is up to date with the times, educating the youth about their potential within the digital future and their potential in all other fields of growing interest. We wish to keep our education as the primary driving motivation and not focus on making money from parents and families.

Our facilities and educational systems will educate the youth on important life topics such as; oil & gas, diet, taxation, time and self management and more vital topics for survival in the real world.

Housing / Apartments

Apartments and town houses will be built within a secure estate, allowing maximum security for residents. This will also cater for commercial activities that would have been set up in the commercial area / shopping centers.

The housing facilities will be state of the art, combining latest technologies with modern day interior design, birthing a futuristic design that can accomodate people of this generation and future generations to come.

Shopping Center

We plan to bring fully functional shopping centers with banks, both high class and affordable clothing stores, wifi access throughout the mall, famous cuisine stores in our food markets, cinemas and much more.

We intend to make the shopping center a one stop shop for all who live in the close by estates and houses, offering delivery services and a full range of shopping necessities.

5 Star Hotel Project

With the construction of the new international airport near hoima, we can expect to see an influx of tourists and travelers. We intend to provide no less than the best hospitality services with well trained staff.

The hotel is currently planned to be situated near the golf course. Hoima currently has no hotel of a rating of 4 stars or higher. The hotel will include conference facilities, allowing businessmen and oil investors to meet and plan.

Golf Course Project

We plan to build an 18 hole golf course. The golf course will be situated near the road that leads to Kampala, allowing easy access to those coming from the current capital.

Next to the golf course we have planned to build a 5 star hotel, allowing both locals and foreigners looking for an escape from the noise of the city to partake in a high class golfing experience.

Sports Arena / Motorcross

We are aiming to build a 10,000 seater sports arena with state of the art technologies and design. This will open Hoima up to the posibility of hosting major sporting events, boosting not only the tourism sector but also encouraging youth in the community to reach the heights of world renown sports heroes.

Alongside the arena, we are also building a motocross track in partnership with another upcoming track in Kenya. This will allow the communication and organisation of local participants in the East African Motocross Games.

Hemp Project

The hemp industry is one of the globes fastest growing industries at this point in time. The medicinal benefits of hemp products, coupled with the flexibility of it’s use makes hemp one of the world’s most widely usable plants.

Hemp products range from textile, medicine, energy, building material, food and much more. With the world slowly realising the benefits of hemp, the Y.M.F is dedicated towards doing the adequate research into this plant, with the hopes of providing future generations with a more naturalistic lifestyle.

Bamboo Project

With three of our members already part of the Uganda Bamboo Association, we are looking to plant up to 10 acres worth of land with strictly bamboo. We will use this bamboo for the construction of many beautiful buildings, including our own eco lodge project. Bamboo is a rapidly growing industry, as bamboo has multiple uses ranging from the manufacturing of goods to providing energy, and even as feed for our livestock.

Museum Project

The Y.M.F is working to build a museum with a deep and rich collection of historical artifacts and information. The founder, Mr Yesero Mugenyi, is a Mubiito (a prince from the Royal Clan).

We are hoping to delve into Africa’s rich history and discover not only the depth of our Ugandan roots, but all the way back into the ancient roots of Ancient Ethiopia, who’s Kingdom once encompassed the entire Eastern Africa.

Library Project

The founder of the Y.M.F has already managed to accumulate a large variety of books, mainly law books. He was the pillar of his families education; very intellectual and loved to read, passing the gene all the way down to his grandchildren.

5 of his 6 children went on to become lawyers, with one going on to reach the honorary title of a judge. The 6th and youngest of his children, inspired by her father, went on to become business orientated and ventured into the airline industry: reaching the title of Country Manager for 4 airlines.

Combining books collected by him, his children and his grandchildren, we have already accumulated a large number of books that are applicable to people of all generations. We are aiming at not only building a physical library but a digital library, with audiobooks.

We intend to erect a library with multiple floors, extending not only upwards but also underground.

Technology Projects

The digital revolution has provided the Y.M.F with a unique opportunity to advance into the future with the hopes of building an interconnected networking system throughout our satellite town. This system will help support farming, communication, security, electricity and water supply, transport and much more.

With research being done into green technology, alongside our understanding of the dangers of radiation emitted from networking frequencies, we are equipped with the knowledge and expertise to find a peaceful center point between nature and technology. This will ensure that we are not replacing the old ways of farming and living, but rather building upon them to create a more efficient and effective way for all communities within the town to have equal access to both advanced technology, and back to basic lifestyles.

We will strive to keep our town up to date with the latest technologies, after carefully analyzing the health risks they may place upon the members of the community.
